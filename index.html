स्तोत्र संग्रह

Stotra Sangrah: A Collection of Sacred Divine Hymns

श्री विष्णु सहस्रनाम

पूर्वभागः (Introductory Prayers)

These verses are chanted to invoke the protection of the Lord and the guidance of the Sages.

शुक्लाम्बरधरं विष्णुं शशिवर्णं चतुर्भुजम् ।
प्रसन्नवदनं ध्यायेत् सर्वविघ्नोपशान्तये ॥
Meaning: We meditate upon Lord Vishnu, who is clad in white robes, who has the color of the moon, who has four arms, and who has a serene, pleasant face; may He remove all obstacles.
व्यासं वसिष्ठनप्तारं शक्तेः पौत्रमकल्मषम् ।
पराशरात्मजं वन्दे शुकतातं तपोनिधिम् ॥
Meaning: I salute Sage Vyasa, the great-grandson of Vasistha, the grandson of Shaktri, the son of Parashara, and the father of Shuka—the pure treasure of penance.
व्यासाय विष्णुरूपाय व्यासरूपाय विष्णवे ।
नमो वै ब्रह्मनिधये वासिष्ठाय नमो नमः ॥
Meaning: Salutations to Vyasa, who is a form of Vishnu, and to Vishnu, who is a form of Vyasa. I bow to the descendant of Vasistha, who is a treasure of Vedic knowledge.
अविकाराय शुद्धाय नित्याय परमात्मने ।
सदैकरूपरूपाय विष्णवे सर्वजिष्णवे ॥
Meaning: Salutations to Vishnu, who is beyond change, who is pure and eternal, who is the Supreme Soul, and who is ever-victorious over all.
किमेकं दैवतं लोके किं वाप्येकं परायणम् ।
स्तुवन्तः कं कमर्चन्तः प्राप्नुयुर्मानवाः शुभम् ॥
Meaning: (King Yudhishthira asks:) "Who is the one Deity in the world? What is the one supreme goal? By praising and worshipping whom do men attain auspiciousness and peace?"
जगत्प्रभुं देवदेवमनन्तं पुरुषोत्तमम् ।
स्तुवन्नामसहस्रेण पुरुषः सततोत्थितः ॥
Meaning: (Bhishma answers:) "By always praising the Lord of the Universe, the God of Gods, the Infinite One, and the Supreme Person through His thousand names, one transcends all worldly sorrows."

स्तोत्रम् (The Thousand Names)

The first name is Vishwa (The Universe), and the last name is Sarvapraharanayudha (He who uses everything as a weapon for protection).

Verse 1
विश्वं विष्णुर्वषट्कारो भूतभव्यभवत्प्रभुः ।
भूतकृद्भूतभृद्भावो भूतात्मा भूतभावनः ॥ १ ॥
Meaning: He is the Universe itself, the Pervader of all, the one invoked in sacrifices. He is the Lord of the past, present, and future. He is the Creator of all beings, the Sustainer, the Essence of all existence, and the One who causes all beings to flourish.
Verse 2
पूतात्मा परमात्मा च मुक्तानां परमा गतिः ।
अव्ययः पुरुषः साक्षी क्षेत्रज्ञोऽक्षर एव च ॥ २ ॥
Meaning: The Pure Soul, the Supreme Soul, the ultimate goal of liberated souls. He is the Imperishable, the Indwelling Spirit, the Witness of all actions, the Knower of the field (body/matter), and the Undecaying one.
Verse 3
योगो योगविदां नेता प्रधानपुरुषेश्वरः ।
नारसिंहवपुः श्रीमान् केशवः पुरुषोत्तमः ॥ ३ ॥
Meaning: The goal of Yoga, the leader of those who know Yoga, the Lord of Nature and Spirit. He who assumed the Man-Lion form, the possessor of Shri (Lakshmi), the one with beautiful hair (or slayer of Keshi), and the Supreme Person.
Verse 4
सर्वः शर्वः शिवः स्थाणुर्भूतादिर्निधिरव्ययः ।
सम्भवो भावनो भर्ता प्रभवः प्रभुिरीश्वरः ॥ ४ ॥
Meaning: He is everything, the Remover of sorrows, the Auspicious one, the Immovable, the Source of all elements, and the Inexhaustible Treasure. He manifests by His own will, is the Giver of everything, the Supporter, and the Lord of all power.
Verse 5
स्वयम्भूः शम्भुरादित्यः पुष्कराक्षो महास्वनः ।
अनादिनिधनो धाता विधाता धातुरुत्तमः ॥ ५ ॥
Meaning: Self-existent, the source of happiness, the Sun, the lotus-eyed, and the possessor of the Great Sound (Vedas). He has no beginning or end, is the Support of the universe, the Dispenser of fruits of action, and the Supreme Substratum.
Verse 6
अप्रमेयो हृषीकेशः पद्मनाभोऽमरप्रभुः ।
विश्वकर्मा मनुस्त्वष्टा स्थविष्ठः स्थविरो ध्रुवः ॥ ६ ॥
Meaning: The Unfathomable, Lord of the senses, He from whose navel the lotus of creation springs, and the Lord of the Immortals. He is the Architect of the universe, the Thinker, the Distiller of forms, the Most Massive, the Ancient, and the Eternal.
Verse 7
अग्राह्यः शाश्वतः कृष्णो लोहिताक्षः प्रतर्दनः ।
प्रभूतस्त्रिककुब्धाम पवित्रं मङ्गलं परम् ॥ ७ ॥
Meaning: He who cannot be grasped by the senses, the Eternal, the All-Attractive (Krishna), the Red-eyed, and the Destroyer of all during Pralaya. He is full of riches, the Support of the three regions, the Purest, and the Supreme Auspiciousness.
Verse 8
ईशानः प्राणदः प्राणो ज्येष्ठः श्रेष्ठः प्रजापतिः ।
हिरण्यगर्भो भूगर्भो माधवो मधुसूदनः ॥ ८ ॥
Meaning: The Ruler of all, the Giver of life, Life itself, the Eldest, the Best, and the Lord of all creatures. He is the Golden Womb, the one who protects the Earth, the Consort of Lakshmi (Madhava), and the Slayer of the demon Madhu.
Verse 9
ईश्वरो विक्रमी धन्वी मेधावी विक्रमः क्रमः ।
अनुत्तमो दुराधर्षः कृतज्ञः कृतिरात्मवान् ॥ ९ ॥
Meaning: The Almighty, the Valiant, the wielder of the Bow, the highly Intelligent, the Pervader, and the Orderly progression of the world. He is the Peerless, the Unconquerable, the Grateful, the Rewarder of deeds, and the Master of the Self.
Verse 10
सुरेशः शरणं शर्म विश्वरेताः प्रजाभवः ।
अहः संवत्सरो व्यालः प्रत्ययः सर्वदर्शनः ॥ १० ॥
Meaning: Lord of the Gods, the ultimate Refuge, the Embodiment of Bliss, the Seed of the Universe, and the Origin of all. He is Time (Day and Year), the Unseizable, the Consciousness, and the All-Seeing.
Verse 11
अजः सर्वेश्वरः सिद्धः सिद्धिः सर्वादिरच्युतः ।
वृषाकपिरमेयात्मा सर्वयोगविनिस्सृतः ॥ ११ ॥
Meaning: The Unborn, Lord of all, the Perfect, the Perfection, the Beginning of everything, and the Infallible. He is the Dharma-boar, the Unmeasurable Soul, and the one realized through all Yogas.
Verse 12
वसुर्वसुमनाः सत्यः समात्माऽसम्मितः समः ।
अमोघः पुण्डरीकाक्षो वृषकर्मा वृषाकृतिः ॥ १२ ॥
Meaning: The Dwelling-place of all, the Wealthy-minded, the Truth, the Equal-souled, the Immeasurable, and the Uniform. His actions are never fruitless, He is the Lotus-eyed, of righteous deeds, and of the form of Dharma.
Verse 13
रुद्रो बहुशिरा बभ्रुर्विश्वयोनिः शुचिश्रवाः ।
अमृतः शाश्वतस्थाणुर्वरारोहो महातपाः ॥ १३ ॥
Meaning: He who makes others cry (at the end of time), the Many-headed, the Supporter, the Womb of the Universe, and the one of pure fame. He is the Nectar, the Eternal Pillar, the most Excellent, and the Supreme Ascetic.
Verse 14
सर्वगः सर्वविद्भानुर्विष्वक्सेनो जनार्दनः ।
वेदो वेदविदव्यङ्गो वेदाङ्गो वेदवित्कविः ॥ १४ ॥
Meaning: The All-pervading, the All-knowing, the Shining Sun, the one who scatters enemies, and the Helper of all. He is the Vedas, the Knower of Vedas, the Flawless, the parts of Vedas, and the Sage.
Verse 15
लोकाध्यक्षः सुराध्यक्षो धर्माध्यक्षः कृताकृतः ।
चतुरात्मा चतुर्व्यूहश्चतुर्दंष्ट्रश्चतुर्भुजः ॥ १५ ॥
Meaning: Lord of the worlds, Lord of the Devas, Lord of Dharma, and the Cause and Effect. He is the four-fold Soul, the four-fold Manifestation, the four-tusked, and the four-armed.
Verse 16
भ्राजिष्णुर्भोजनं भोक्ता सहिष्णुर्जगदादिजः ।
अनघो विजयो जेता विश्वयोनिः पुनर्वसुः ॥ १६ ॥
Meaning: The Effulgent, the Object of enjoyment, the Enjoyer, the Forbearing, and the First-born of the world. He is Sinless, Victory personified, the Conqueror, the Source of the world, and the Dweller in all bodies.
Verse 17
उपेन्द्रो वामनः प्रांशुरमोघः शुचिरूर्जितः ।
अतीन्द्रः सङ्ग्रहः सर्गो धृतात्मा नियमो यमः ॥ १७ ॥
Meaning: The younger brother of Indra, the Dwarf, the Tall, the Unfailing, the Pure, and the Mighty. He is beyond the senses, the Controller, the Creation, the Self-sustained, and the Final Law.
Verse 18
वेद्यो वैद्यः सदायोगी वीरहा माधवो मधुः ।
अतीन्द्रियो महामायो महोत्साहो महाबलः ॥ १८ ॥
Meaning: The One to be known, the Supreme Physician, the Eternal Yogi, the Destroyer of demons, the Lord of Lakshmi, and the Sweetness. He is beyond perception, the Great Magician, the Great Effort, and the Great Power.
Verse 19
महाबुद्धिर्महावीर्यो महाशक्तिर्महाद्युतिः ।
अनिर्देश्यवपुः श्रीमानमेयात्मा महाद्रिधृक् ॥ १९ ॥
Meaning: The Great Intelligence, the Great Valour, the Great Energy, and the Great Radiance. His form is Indescribable, He is the Possessor of Shri, the Unmeasurable Soul, and the Lifter of the Great Mountain (Govardhana).
Verse 20
महेष्वासो महीभर्ता श्रीनिवासः सतां गतिः ।
अनिरुद्धः सुरानन्दो गोविन्दो गोविदां पतिः ॥ २० ॥
Meaning: The Wielder of the great bow, the Bearer of the Earth, the Abode of Lakshmi, and the Refuge of the virtuous. He is the Unobstructed, the Delight of the Gods, the Chief of Cowherds, and the Lord of the Knowers of Vedas.
Verse 21
मरीचिर्दमनो हंसः सुपर्णो भुजगोत्तमः ।
हिरण्यनाभः सुतपाः पद्मनाभः प्रजापतिः ॥ २१ ॥
Meaning: The Ray of Light, the Controller, the Swan (Soul), the beautifully-winged (Garuda), and the Master of Serpents (Ananta). He of the golden navel, the performer of glorious penance, the lotus-navelled, and the Lord of all creatures.
Verse 22
अमृत्युः सर्वदृक् सिंहः सन्धाता सन्धिमान् स्थिरः ।
अजो दुर्मर्षणः शास्ता विश्रुतात्मा सुरारिहा ॥ २२ ॥
Meaning: Beyond Death, the All-seer, the Lion, the Regulator, the United, and the Fixed. He is the Unborn, the Unbearable to enemies, the Teacher, the Renowned Soul, and the Destroyer of the enemies of the Devas.
Verse 23
गुरुर्गुरुतमो धाम सत्यः सत्यपराक्रमः ।
निमिषोऽनिमिषः स्रग्वी वाचस्पतिरुदारधीः ॥ २३ ॥
Meaning: The Teacher, the Greatest of Teachers, the Abode, the Truth, and the one of Truthful Prowess. He is the one who closes His eyes (in Yoga-Nidra) and the one who never blinks, the wearer of the garland, the Lord of Speech, and the one of Vast Intellect.
Verse 24
अग्रणीग्रामणीः श्रीमान् न्यायो नेता समीरणः ।
सहस्रमूर्धा विश्वात्मा सहस्राक्षः सहस्रपात् ॥ २४ ॥
Meaning: The Leader, the Guide of the world, the Possessor of Shri, Justice itself, the Conductor, and the Breath (Air). He has a thousand heads, is the Soul of the universe, has a thousand eyes, and a thousand feet.
Verse 25
आवर्त्तनो निवृत्तात्मा संवृतः सम्प्रमर्दनः ।
अहः संवर्त्तको वह्निरनिलो धरणीधरः ॥ २५ ॥
Meaning: The Turner of the wheel of life, the Soul withdrawn from matter, the Veiled, and the Subduer. He is the Sun (regulator of days), the Fire, the Air, and the Supporter of the Earth.
Verse 26
सुप्रसादः प्रसन्नात्मा विश्वधृग्विश्वभुग्विभुः ।
सत्कर्त्ता सत्कृतः साधुर्जह्नुर्नारायणो नरः ॥ २६ ॥
Meaning: The Bestower of Grace, the Ever-pure Mind, the Upholder of the world, the Consumer of the world, and the All-pervading. The Doer of good, the Honored one, the Righteous, the Leader of men, the Refuge of all (Narayana), and the Imperishable.
Verse 27
असंख्येयोऽप्रमेयात्मा विशिष्टः शिष्टकृच्छुचिः ।
सिद्धार्थः सिद्धसङ्कल्पः सिद्धिदः सिद्धिसाधनः ॥ २७ ॥
Meaning: The Innumerable, the Unfathomable Soul, the Distinguished, the Maker of laws, and the Pure. He who has achieved all goals, whose Resolve is always fulfilled, the Giver of success, and the Means to success.
Verse 28
वृषाही वृषभो विष्णुर्वृषपर्वा वृषोदरः ।
वर्धनो वर्धमानश्च विविक्तः श्रुतिसागरः ॥ २८ ॥
Meaning: The Lord of auspicious days, the showering of boons (Bull), the Pervader, the steps of Dharma, and the one of large-heartedness. The Increaser, the one who grows, the Solitary, and the Ocean of the Vedas.
Verse 29
सुभुजो दुर्धरो वाग्मी महेन्द्रो वसुदो वसुः ।
नैकरूपो बृहद्रूपः शिपिविष्टः प्रकाशनः ॥ २९ ॥
Meaning: The beautiful-armed, the hard to contain, the eloquent, the Great Lord, the Giver of wealth, and the Wealth itself. He of manifold forms, of the Vast form, the dweller in the rays of light, and the Illuminator.
Verse 30
ओजस्तेजोद्युतिधरः प्रकाशत्मा प्रतापनः ।
ऋद्धः स्पष्टाक्षरो मन्त्रश्चन्द्रांशुर्भास्करद्युतिः ॥ ३० ॥
Meaning: The possessor of Vitality, Splendor, and Radiance. The Radiant Soul, the Heat (Sun), the Prosperous, the Clear-lettered (Om), the Sacred Mantra, the cool rays of the Moon, and the brilliance of the Sun.
Verse 31
अमृतांशूद्भवो भानुः शशबिन्दुः सुरेश्वरः ।
औषधं जगतः सेतुः सत्यधर्मपराक्रमः ॥ ३१ ॥
Meaning: The source of the moon's nectar, the Sun, the moon (spotted like a rabbit), the Lord of Devas, the Medicine for the world's ills, the Bridge across existence, and the one whose prowess is Truth and Dharma.
Verse 32
भूतभव्यभवन्नाथः पवनः पावनोऽनलः ।
कामहा कामकृत्कान्तः कामः कामप्रदः प्रभुः ॥ ३२ ॥
Meaning: The Lord of past, present, and future. The Wind, the Purifier, the Fire, the Destroyer of desires, the Creator of desires, the Charming, the Desired, and the Bestower of desires.
Verse 33
युगादिकृद्युगावर्त्तो नैकमायो महाशनः ।
अदृश्यो व्यक्तरूपश्च सहस्रजिदनन्तजित् ॥ ३३ ॥
Meaning: The Creator of the Yugas, the Turner of the Yugas, the Master of diverse illusions, and the Consumer of all things (at Pralaya). The Invisible, the Manifested, the Conqueror of thousands, and the infinitely Victorious.
Verse 34
इष्टोऽविशिष्टः शिष्टेष्टः शिखण्डी नहुषो वृषः ।
क्रोधहा क्रोधकृत्कर्त्ता विश्वबाहुर्महीधरः ॥ ३४ ॥
Meaning: The Beloved, the One within all, the beloved of the wise, the wearer of the peacock feather (Krishna), the binder of souls, and the Dharma. The Destroyer of anger, the Creator of anger (against evil), the Doer, the Arms of the Universe, and the Supporter of the Earth.
Verse 35
अच्युतः प्रथितः प्राणः प्राणदो वासवानुजः ।
अपांनिधिरधिष्ठानमप्रमत्तः प्रतिष्ठितः ॥ ३५ ॥
Meaning: The Infallible, the Celebrated, the Life-breath, the Giver of life, and the younger brother of Indra (Upendra). The Ocean, the Substratum, the Vigilant, and the Self-established.
Verse 36
स्कन्दः स्कन्दधरो धुर्यो वरदो वायुवाहनः ।
वासुदेवो बृहद्भानुरादिदेवः पुरन्धरः ॥ ३६ ॥
Meaning: The Effusion (or Kartikeya), the Supporter of the path of righteousness, the Bearer of weights, the Giver of boons, and the one for whom the wind is a vehicle. The son of Vasudeva, the Great Brilliance, the First Deity, and the Destroyer of cities.
Verse 37
अशोकस्तारणस्तारः शूरः शौरिर्जनेश्वरः ।
अनुकूलः शतावर्त्तः पद्मी पद्मनिभेक्षणः ॥ ३७ ॥
Meaning: The Sorrowless, the Savior, the Star, the Hero, the son of Shura, and the Lord of people. The Compliant, the one of a hundred manifestations, the holder of the lotus, and the lotus-eyed.
Verse 38
पद्मनाभोऽरविन्दाक्षः पद्मगर्भः शरीरभृत् ।
महर्द्धिर्ऋद्धो वृद्धात्मा महाक्षो गरुडध्वजः ॥ ३८ ॥
Meaning: The lotus-navelled, the lotus-eyed, the one residing in the lotus of the heart, and the Sustainer of bodies. The greatly Prosperous, the Flourishing, the Ancient Soul, the great-eyed, and He who has Garuda on His flag.
Verse 39
अतुलः शरभो भीमः समयज्ञो हविर्हरिः ।
सर्वलक्षणलक्षण्यो लक्ष्मीवान् समितिञ्जयः ॥ ३९ ॥
Meaning: The Incomparable, the Destroyer of those who cross the path of Dharma, the Terrible (to evil), the Knower of time, and the Receiver of offerings. He who is known by all signs of greatness, the Consort of Lakshmi, and the Victor in battle.
Verse 40
विक्षरो रोहितो मार्गो हेतुर्दामोदरः सहः ।
महीधरो महाभागो वेगवानमिताशनः ॥ ४० ॥
Meaning: The Imperishable, the Red-hued (Fish incarnation), the Path, the Cause, and the one tied by a rope (Damodara). The Patient, the Supporter of the Earth, the Fortunate, the Swift, and the Eater of immense quantities (during Pralaya).

उद्भवः क्षोभणो देवः श्रीगर्भः परमेश्वरः ।
करणं कारणं कर्ता विकर्ता गहनो गुहः ॥ ४१ ॥
Meaning: The Originator, the Agitator (of Prakriti), the Radiant One, the one containing all glory, and the Supreme Lord. He is the Instrument, the Cause, the Doer, the Creator of variations, the Unfathomable, and the Dweller in the cave of the heart.
Verse 42
व्यवसायो व्यवस्थानः संस्थानः स्थानदो ध्रुवः ।
परर्द्धिः परमस्पष्टस्तुष्टः पुष्टः शुभेक्षणः ॥ ४२ ॥
Meaning: The Resolve, the Orderer, the ultimate Foundation, the Giver of status, and the Eternal. He is the Supreme Prosperity, the most Manifest, the Contented, the Full, and the one of auspicious gaze.
Verse 43
रामो विरामो विरजो मार्गो नेयो नयोऽनयः ।
वीरः शक्तिमतां श्रेष्ठो धर्मो धर्मविदुत्तमः ॥ ४३ ॥
Meaning: The One who delights (Rama), the End of all things, the Passionless, the Way, the Led, the Leader, and the Law. The Hero, the Best among the powerful, the Dharma, and the Greatest among those who know Dharma.
Verse 44
वैकुण्ठः पुरुषः प्राणः प्राणदः प्रणवः पृथुः ।
हिरण्यगर्भः शत्रुघ्नः व्याप्तो वायुरधोक्षजः ॥ ४४ ॥
Meaning: The Remover of obstacles, the Spirit, the Life, the Giver of life, the sacred syllable 'Om', and the Expanded. The Golden Womb, the Destroyer of enemies, the Pervader, the Air, and the one who never falls away.
Verse 45
ऋतुः सुदर्शनः कालः परमेष्ठी परिग्रहः ।
उग्रः संवत्सरो दक्षो विश्रामो विश्वदक्षिणः ॥ ४५ ॥
Meaning: The Seasons, the one with auspicious vision, the Time, the Supreme Dweller, and the Receiver of offerings. The Terrible (to the wicked), the Year, the Skillful, the Resting-place, and the most Efficient in the world.
Verse 46
विस्तारः स्थावरस्थाणुः प्रमाणं बीजमव्ययम् ।
अर्थोऽनर्थो महाकोशो महाभोगो महाधनः ॥ ४६ ॥
Meaning: The Expansion, the Stable and Motionless, the Authority, and the Imperishable Seed. The Goal, the one without desire, the Great Sheath, the Great Enjoyment, and the Great Wealth.
Verse 47
अनिर्विण्णः स्थविष्ठोऽभूर्धर्मयूपो महामखः ।
नक्षत्रनेमिर्नक्षत्री क्षमः क्षामः समीहनः ॥ ४७ ॥
Meaning: The Untiring, the extremely Large, the Unborn, the Pillar of Dharma, and the Great Sacrifice. The Hub of the stars, the Master of stars, the Patient, the Remaining, and the Desirer of the world's welfare.
Verse 48
यज्ञ इज्यो महेज्यश्च क्रतुः सत्रं सतां गतिः ।
सर्वदर्शी विमुक्तात्मा सर्वज्ञो ज्ञानमुत्तमम् ॥ ४८ ॥
Meaning: The Sacrifice, the Worshipful, the most Worshipful, the Ritual, the Protector of the good, and the Refuge of the virtuous. The All-seer, the Liberated Soul, the All-knowing, and the Supreme Knowledge.
Verse 49
सुव्रतः सुमुखः सूक्ष्मः सुघोषः सुखदः सुहृत् ।
मनोहरो जितक्रोधो वीरबाहुर्विदारणः ॥ ४९ ॥
Meaning: The one of pure vows, the beautiful-faced, the Subtle, the auspicious Sound, the Giver of happiness, and the Friend. The Captivator of the mind, the Conqueror of anger, the valiant-armed, and the Destroyer of evil.
Verse 50
स्वापनः स्ववशो व्यापी नैकात्मा नैककर्मकृत् ।
वत्सरो वत्सलो वत्सी रत्नगर्भो धनेश्वरः ॥ ५० ॥
Meaning: The one who puts to sleep (Pralaya), the Independent, the Pervading, the many-formed, and the Performer of many deeds. The Abode, the Affectionate, the Father (of all), the Jewel-womb, and the Lord of wealth.
Verse 51
धर्मगुब्धर्मकृद्धर्मी सदसत्क्षरमक्षरम् ।
अविज्ञाता सहस्रांशुर्विधाता कृतलक्षणः ॥ ५१ ॥
Meaning: The Protector of Dharma, the Performer of Dharma, the righteous, the Being and Non-being, the Perishable and Imperishable. The Unknown, the Thousand-rayed Sun, the Ordainer, and the one of famous attributes.
Verse 52
गभस्तिनेमिः सत्त्वस्थः सिंहो भूतमहेश्वरः ।
आदिदेवो महादेवो देवेशो देवभृद्गुरुः ॥ ५२ ॥
Meaning: The center of light, established in Truth, the Lion, the Great Lord of all beings. The First Deity, the Great Deity, the Lord of Devas, and the Teacher of the Devas.
Verse 53
उत्तरो गोपतिर्गोप्ता ज्ञानगम्यः पुरातनः ।
शरीरभूतभृद्भोक्ता कपीन्द्रो भूरिदक्षिणः ॥ ५३ ॥
Meaning: The Rescuer, the Lord of cows/senses, the Protector, the one reached through knowledge, and the Ancient. The Supporter of the physical body, the Enjoyer, the Lord of monkeys (Rama/Hanuman), and the Giver of great gifts.
Verse 54
सोमपोऽमृतपः सोमः पुरुजित्पुरुसत्तमः ।
विनयो जयः सत्यसन्धो दाशार्हः सात्त्वतां पतिः ॥ ५४ ॥
Meaning: The Drinker of Soma, the Drinker of nectar, the Moon, the Conqueror of many, and the Best among many. The Humility, the Victory, the Truth-bound, the descendant of Dasharha, and the Lord of the Sattvatas.
Verse 55
जीवो विनयितासाक्षी मुकुन्दोऽमितविक्रमः ।
अम्भोनिधिरनन्तात्मा महोदधिशयोऽन्तकः ॥ ५५ ॥
Meaning: The Life-force, the Witness of humility, the Giver of liberation (Mukunda), and the one of immeasurable prowess. The Ocean, the Infinite Soul, the one who sleeps on the great ocean, and the Ender.
Verse 56
अजो महार्हः स्वाभाव्यो जितामित्रः प्रमोदनः ।
आनन्दो नन्दनो नन्दः सत्यधर्मा त्रिविक्रमः ॥ ५६ ॥
Meaning: The Unborn, the Deserving of worship, the Self-established, the Vanquisher of enemies, and the Ever-joyful. The Bliss, the Giver of joy, the Prosperity, the Truthful Dharma, and the Conqueror of the three worlds.
Verse 57
महर्षिः कपिलाचार्यः कृतज्ञो मेदिनीपतिः ।
त्रिपदस्त्रिदशाध्यक्षो महाशृङ्गः कृतान्तकृत् ॥ ५७ ॥
Meaning: The Great Sage (Kapila), the Grateful, and the Lord of the Earth. The one of three steps (Vamana), the Lord of the thirty gods (Devas), the Great-horned (Fish incarnation), and the Destroyer of Death.
Verse 58
महावराहो गोविन्दः सुषेणः कनकाङ्गदी ।
गुह्यो गभीरो गहनो गुप्तश्चक्रगदाधरः ॥ ५८ ॥
Meaning: The Great Boar, the Knower of Vedas, the one with a perfect army, and the wearer of golden armlets. The Secret, the Deep, the Inaccessible, the Hidden, and the wielder of the Discus and Mace.
Verse 59
वेधाः स्वाङ्गोऽजितः कृष्णो दृढः संकर्षणोऽच्युतः ।
वरुणो वारुणो वृक्षः पुष्कराक्षो महामनाः ॥ ५९ ॥
Meaning: The Creator, the one with beautiful limbs, the Unconquered, the All-attractive, the Firm, the Drawer of all, and the Infallible. The evening Sun, the son of Varuna, the Tree of the world, the lotus-eyed, and the Great-minded.
Verse 60
भगवान् भगहाऽऽनन्दी वनमाली हलायुधः ।
आदित्यो ज्योतिरादित्यः सहिष्णुर्गतिसत्तमः ॥ ६० ॥
Meaning: The Lord of six glories, the Destroyer of those glories (at Pralaya), the Blissful, the wearer of forest garlands, and the wielder of the plow (Balarama). The Sun, the Light within the Sun, the Forbearing, and the Supreme Refuge.
Verse 61
सुधन्वा खण्डपरशुर्दारुणो द्रविणप्रदः ।
दिवस्पृक् सर्वदृग्व्यासो वाचस्पतिरयोनिजः ॥ ६१ ॥
Meaning: The one with a splendid bow, the wielder of the sharp axe (Parashurama), the Terrible (to evil), and the Giver of wealth. He touches the heavens, sees everything, is the Divider of Vedas (Vyasa), the Lord of speech, and the Unborn.
Verse 62
त्रिसामा सामगः साम निर्वाणं भेषजं भिषक् ।
संन्यासकृच्छमः शान्तो निष्ठा शान्तिः परायणम् ॥ ६२ ॥
Meaning: The one praised by the three Samas, the Singer of Samas, and the Sama Veda itself. He is the Liberation, the Medicine for life's ills, the Physician, the ordainer of Sannyasa, the Calm, the Peaceful, the ultimate Reality, the Peace, and the Supreme Goal.
Verse 63
शुभाङ्गः शान्तिदः स्रष्टा कुमुदः कुवलेशयः ।
गोहितो गोपतिर्गोप्ता वृषभाक्षो वृषप्रियः ॥ ६३ ॥
Meaning: The one with auspicious limbs, the Giver of peace, the Creator, the one who delights in the Earth, and the one who reclines on the waters. He is the Well-wisher of cows/senses, the Lord of the world, the Protector, the one with eyes like a bull (Dharma), and the lover of righteousness.
Verse 64
अनिवर्ती निवृत्तात्मा संक्षेप्ता क्षेमकृच्छिवः ।
श्रीवत्सवक्षाः श्रीवासः श्रीपतिः श्रीमतां वरः ॥ ६४ ॥
Meaning: The one who never retreats, the soul withdrawn from matter, the one who contracts the universe (at Pralaya), the Giver of well-being, and the Auspicious. He who has the Shrivatsa mark on His chest, the abode of Shri, the Lord of Lakshmi, and the best among the glorious.
Verse 65
श्रीदः श्रीशः श्रीनिवासः श्रीनिधिः श्रीविभावनः ।
श्रीधरः श्रीकरः श्रेयः श्रीमाँल्लोकत्रयाश्रयः ॥ ६५ ॥
Meaning: The Giver of wealth, the Lord of wealth, the Abode of wealth, the Treasure of wealth, and the Distributor of wealth. He who holds Lakshmi on His chest, the creator of prosperity, the Supreme Good, the possessor of Shri, and the refuge of the three worlds.
Verse 66
स्वक्षः स्वङ्गः शतानन्दो नन्दिर्ज्योतिर्गणेश्वरः ।
विजितात्मा विधेयात्मा सत्कीर्तिश्छिन्नसंशयः ॥ ६६ ॥
Meaning: The one with beautiful eyes, beautiful limbs, and infinite joy. He is Prosperity itself, the Lord of the luminaries (stars/planets), the one who has conquered His mind, the self-controlled, the one of true fame, and the destroyer of doubts.
Verse 67
उदीर्णः सर्वतश्चक्षुरनीशः शाश्वतस्थिरः ।
भूशयो भूषणो भूतिर्विशोकः शोकनाशनः ॥ ६७ ॥
Meaning: The Manifested, the one with eyes everywhere, the one who has no master (Supreme), and the eternally Stable. He who slept on the ground (Rama), the Ornament of the world, the Existence, the Sorrowless, and the Destroyer of sorrow.
Verse 68
अर्चिष्मानर्चितः कुम्भो विशुद्धात्मा विशोधनः ।
अनिरुद्धोऽप्रतिरथः प्रद्युम्नोऽमितविक्रमः ॥ ६८ ॥
Meaning: The Effulgent, the Worshipped, the Pot (containing everything), the Pure Soul, and the Purifier. The Unobstructed, the one without a rival, the Illumined, and the one of immeasurable prowess.
Verse 69
कालनेमिनिहा वीरः शौरिः शूरजनेश्वरः ।
त्रिलोकात्मा त्रिलोकेशः केशवः केशिहा हरिः ॥ ६९ ॥
Meaning: The Slayer of the demon Kalanemi, the Hero, the son of Shura, and the Lord of heroes. The Soul of the three worlds, the Lord of the three worlds, the Master of Brahma and Shiva, the slayer of Keshi, and the Remover of sins.
Verse 70
कामदेवः कामपालः कामी कान्तः कृतागमः ।
अनिर्देश्यवपुर्विष्णुर्वीरोऽनन्तो धनञ्जयः ॥ ७० ॥
Meaning: The God of love, the Protector of desires, the one of fulfilled desires, the Charming, and the author of the Vedas. He of indefinable form, the Pervader, the Valiant, the Infinite, and the Winner of wealth (Arjuna/Vishnu).
Verse 71
ब्रह्मण्यो ब्रह्मकृद्ब्रह्मा ब्रह्म ब्रह्मविवर्धनः ।
ब्रह्मविद्ब्राह्मणो ब्रह्मी ब्रह्मज्ञो ब्राह्मणप्रियः ॥ ७१ ॥
Meaning: The Friend of Brahman, the Creator, the great Brahma, the Absolute Reality, and the one who promotes Vedic knowledge. The knower of Brahman, the Brahman, the possessor of Brahman, the understander of Brahman, and the one dear to the devotees of Brahman.
Verse 72
महाक्रमो महाकर्मा महातेजा महोरगः ।
महाक्रतुर्महायज्वा महायज्ञो महाहविः ॥ ७२ ॥
Meaning: The one of great strides, the performer of great deeds, the possessor of great brilliance, and the Great Serpent (Ananta). The Great Ritual, the Great Sacrificer, the Great Sacrifice, and the Great Offering.
Verse 73
स्तव्यः स्तवप्रियः स्तोत्रं स्तुतिः स्तोता रणप्रियः ।
पूर्णः पूरयिता पुण्यः पुण्यकीर्तिरनामयः ॥ ७३ ॥
Meaning: The one worthy of praise, the lover of praise, the Hymn, the Act of praise, and the Praiser. The lover of battle (against evil), the Complete, the Fulfiller, the Holy, the one of holy fame, and the one free from illness.
Verse 74
मनोजवस्तीर्थकरो वसुरेता वसुप्रदः ।
वसुप्रदो वासुदेवो वसुर्वसुमना हविः ॥ ७४ ॥
Meaning: The one swift as the mind, the teacher of holy places, the Golden-seeded, and the Giver of wealth. The Bestower of riches, the son of Vasudeva, the Abode of all, the wealth-minded, and the Sacrifice.
Verse 75
सद्गतिः सत्कृतिः सत्ता सद्भूतिः सत्परायणः ।
शूरसेनो यदुश्रेष्ठः सन्निवासः सुयामुनः ॥ ७५ ॥
Meaning: The goal of the good, the good action, the Existence, the glorious Being, and the refuge of the truth-seekers. He with a heroic army, the best of the Yadus, the abode of the good, and the one attended by the Yamuna (Krishna).
Verse 76
भूतावासो वासुदेवः सर्वासुनिलयोऽनलः ।
दर्पहा दर्पदो दृप्तो दुर्धरोऽथापराजितः ॥ ७६ ॥
Meaning: The dwelling-place of all beings, the God who covers the world, the home of all life-breaths, and the Fire. The destroyer of pride, the giver of pride (to the righteous), the Blissful, the uncontainable, and the Unconquered.
Verse 77
विश्वमूर्तिर्महामूर्तिर्दीप्तमूर्तिरमूर्तिमान् ।
अनेकमूर्तिरव्यक्तः शतमूर्तिः शताननः ॥ ७७ ॥
Meaning: The cosmic form, the great form, the effulgent form, and the formless. He of many forms, the unmanifested, the one of a hundred forms, and the one of a hundred faces.
Verse 78

लोकबन्धुर्लोकनाथो माधवो भक्तवत्सलः ॥ ७८ ॥
Meaning: The One, the Many, the Sacrifice, the Joy, the 'What' (inquiry), and the 'That' (the goal). The Supreme Abode, the friend of the world, the Lord of the world, the consort of Lakshmi, and the lover of His devotees.
Verse 79
सुवर्णवर्णो हेमाङ्गो वराङ्गश्चन्दनाङ्गदी ।
वीरहा विषमः शून्यो घृताशीरचलश्चलः ॥ ७९ ॥
Meaning: The golden-hued, the golden-limbed, the beautiful-limbed, and the one with sandalwood armlets. The slayer of demons, the Unequalled, the Void (beyond attributes), the one whose desires are fulfilled, the Immovable, and the Movable (as the world).
Verse 80
अमानी मानदो मान्यो लोकस्वामी त्रिलोकधृक् ।
सुमेधा मेधजो धन्यः सत्यमेधा धराधरः ॥ ८० ॥
Meaning: The one without ego, the giver of honor, the worshipful, the master of the world, and the sustainer of the three worlds. The one of good intelligence, born of sacrifice, the Fortunate, the one of true intellect, and the supporter of the Earth.
Verse 81

प्रग्रहो निग्रहो व्यग्रो नैकशृङ्गो गदाग्रजः ॥ ८१ ॥
Meaning: The one who showers radiance, the bearer of effulgence, and the best among those who wield weapons. The one who receives offerings, the one who controls, the ever-active, the one with many horns (in his incarnations), and the elder brother of Gada.
Verse 82
चतुर्मूर्तिश्चतुर्बाहुश्चतुर्व्यूहश्चतुर्गतिः ।
चतुरात्मा चतुर्भावश्चतुर्वेदविदेकपात् ॥ ८२ ॥
Meaning: The one with four forms, four arms, four manifestations, and the four-fold goal. The four-fold soul, the source of the four states of being, the knower of the four Vedas, and the one-footed (the support of all).
Verse 83
समावर्तोऽनिवृत्तात्मा दुर्जयो दुरतिक्रमः ।
दुर्लभो दुर्गमो दुर्गो दुरावासो दुरारिहा ॥ ८३ ॥
Meaning: The turner of the wheel of life, the soul who never retreats from its nature, the hard to conquer, and the hard to resist. The one difficult to attain, difficult to realize, difficult to cross, difficult to house, and the slayer of difficult enemies.
Verse 84
शुभाङ्गो लोकसारङ्गः सुतन्तुस्तन्तुवर्धनः ।
इन्द्रकर्मा महाकर्मा कृतकर्मा कृतागमः ॥ ८४ ॥
Meaning: The one with beautiful limbs, the one who grasps the essence of the world, the one with a beautiful thread (continuity), and the expander of that thread. He of glorious deeds, the great doer, the one who has completed His tasks, and the author of the Vedas.
Verse 85
उद्भवः सुन्दरः सुन्दो रत्ननाभः सुलोचनः ।
अर्को वाजसनः शृङ्गी जयन्तः सर्वविज्जयी ॥ ८५ ॥
Meaning: The one born of his own will, the beautiful, the giver of joy, the jewel-navelled, and the one with beautiful eyes. The Sun, the giver of food, the horned one, the ever-victorious, and the all-knowing victor.
Verse 86

Verse 87
कुमुदः कुन्दरः कुन्दः पर्जन्यः पावनोऽनिलः ।
अमृतांशोऽमृतवपुः सर्वज्ञः सर्वतोमुखः ॥ ८७ ॥
Meaning: The one who delights in the earth, the one who pierced the earth (to bring up water), the giver of pure boons, the rain-cloud, the purifier, and the air. The nectar-rayed moon, the one of immortal form, the all-knowing, and the one with faces everywhere.
Verse 88
सुलभः सुव्रतः सिद्धः शत्रुजिच्छत्रुतापनः ।
न्यग्रोधोऽदुम्बरोऽश्वत्थश्चाणूरान्ध्रनिषूदनः ॥ ८८ ॥
Meaning: The one easily attained (through devotion), the one of pure vows, the perfect, the conqueror of enemies, and the scorcher of foes. The banyan tree, the fig tree, the holy peepal tree, and the slayer of the wrestler Chanura.
Verse 89
सहस्रार्चिः सप्तजिह्वः सप्तैधाः सप्तवाहनः ।
अमूर्तिरनिघोऽचिन्त्यो भयकृद्भयनाशनः ॥ ८९ ॥
Meaning: The one with a thousand rays, the seven-tongued fire, the seven fuels, and the one with seven horses (the Sun). The formless, the flawless, the unthinkable, the creator of fear (for the wicked), and the destroyer of fear (for the devotees).
Verse 90
अणुर्बृहत्कृशः स्थूलो गुणभृन्निर्गुणो महान् ।
अधृतः स्वधृतः स्वास्यः प्राग्वंशो वंशवर्धनः ॥ ९० ॥
Meaning: The atomic, the massive, the lean, the stout, the bearer of attributes, the attribute-less, and the great. The un-supported, the self-supported, the beautiful-faced, the first of dynasties, and the multiplier of the dynasty.
Verse 91
भारभृत्कथितो योगी योगीशः सर्वकामदः ।
आश्रमः श्रमणः क्षामः सुपर्णो वायुवाहनः ॥ ९१ ॥
Meaning: The bearer of the world's weight, the one praised by all, the king of Yogis, and the fulfiller of all desires. The resting-place, the one who purifies the diligent, the one who wanes (during Pralaya), the beautifully-winged, and the one for whom the wind is a vehicle.
Verse 92
धनुर्धरो धनुर्वेदो दण्डो दमयिता दमः ।
अपराजितः सर्वसहो नियन्ताऽनियमोऽयमः ॥ ९२ ॥
Meaning: The wielder of the bow, the science of archery, the scepter of punishment, the punisher, and the punishment itself. The undefeated, the one who bears everything, the controller, the one who is not controlled, and the one who is deathless.
Verse 93
सत्त्ववान् सात्त्विकः सत्यः सत्यधर्मपरायणः ।
अभिप्रायः प्रियार्होऽर्हः प्रियकृत्प्रीतिवर्धनः ॥ ९३ ॥
Meaning: The one full of purity, the virtuous, the truth, and the one devoted to truth and dharma. The goal of all, the one worthy of love, the worshipful, the doer of good, and the increaser of joy.
Verse 94
विहायसगतिर्ज्योतिः सुरुचिर्हुतभुग्विभुः ।
रविर्विरोचनः सूर्यः सविता रविलोचनः ॥ ९४ ॥
Meaning: The one who moves in space, the light, the beautiful splendor, the consumer of offerings, and the all-pervading. The sun, the illuminator, the source of light, the creator, and the one whose eye is the sun.
Verse 95
अनन्तो हुतभुग्भोक्ता सुखदो नैकजोऽग्रजः ।
अनिर्विण्णः सदामर्षी लोकाधिष्ठानमद्भुतः ॥ ९५ ॥
Meaning: The infinite, the consumer of sacrifices, the enjoyer, the giver of happiness, the one born many times, and the first-born. The untiring, the one who forgives the virtuous, the foundation of the world, and the wonderful.
Verse 96
सनात्सनातनतमः कपिलः कपिरव्ययः ।
स्वस्तिदः स्वस्तिकृत्स्वस्ति स्वस्तिभुक्स्वस्तिदक्षिणः ॥ ९६ ॥
Meaning: The beginningless, the most ancient, the sage Kapila, the sun/boar, and the inexhaustible. The giver of well-being, the maker of well-being, well-being itself, the enjoyer of well-being, and the distributor of well-being.
Verse 97
अरौद्रः कुण्डली चक्री विक्रम्यूर्जितशासनः ।
शब्दातिगः शब्दसहः शिशिरः शर्वरीकरः ॥ ९७ ॥
Meaning: The one without anger, the wearer of earrings, the wielder of the discus, the valiant, and the one of firm commands. The one beyond sound, the one who bears all sounds, the cool, and the creator of darkness (night).
Verse 98
अक्रूरः पेशलो दक्षो दक्षिणः क्षमिणां वरः ।
विद्वत्तमो वीतभयः पुण्यश्रवणकीर्तनः ॥ ९८ ॥
Meaning: The non-cruel, the charming, the skillful, the efficient, and the best among the patient. The most learned, the one free from fear, and the one whose names are holy to hear and chant.
Verse 99
उत्तारणो दुष्कृतिहा पुण्यो दुःस्वप्ननाशनः ।
वीरहा रक्षणः सन्तो जीवनः पर्यवस्थितः ॥ ९९ ॥
Meaning: The one who lifts us out of existence, the destroyer of evil deeds, the holy, and the destroyer of bad dreams. The slayer of demons, the protector, the virtuous, the life-force, and the all-pervading.
Verse 100
अनन्तरूपोऽनन्तश्रीर्जितमन्युर्भयापहः ।
चतुरश्रोगभीरात्मा विदिशो व्यादिशो दिशः ॥ १०० ॥
Meaning: The one of infinite forms, infinite glory, the conqueror of anger, and the remover of fear. The one of perfect form, the deep soul, and the one who pervades all directions.
Verse 101
अनादिर्भूर्भुवो लक्ष्मीः सुवीरो रुचिराङ्गदः ।
जननो जनजन्मादिर्भीमो भीमपराक्रमः ॥ १०१ ॥
Meaning: The Beginningless, the support of the Earth, the wealth of the world, the great Hero, and the wearer of beautiful armlets. The Creator of all, the root cause of birth, the Terrible (to evil), and the one of formidable prowess.
Verse 102
आधारनिलयोऽधाता पुष्पहासः प्रजागरः ।
ऊर्ध्वगः सत्पथाचारः प्राणदः प्रणवः पणः ॥ १०२ ॥
Meaning: The fundamental Support, the one who needs no support, the one whose smile is like a blooming flower, and the ever-vigilant. The one who moves upward (beyond matter), the follower of the path of truth, the giver of life, the sacred Om, and the worthiest of all.
Verse 103
प्रमाणं प्राणनिलयः प्राणभृत्प्राणजीवनः ।
तत्त्वं तत्त्वविदेकात्मा जन्ममृत्युजरातिगः ॥ १०३ ॥
Meaning: The ultimate Authority, the resting place of life, the nourisher of life, and the life-force itself. The Truth, the knower of truth, the one Soul, and the one who transcends birth, death, and old age.
Verse 104
भूर्भुवःस्वस्तरुस्तारः सविता प्रपितामहः ।
यज्ञो यज्ञपतिर्यज्वा यज्ञाङ्गो यज्ञवाहनः ॥ १०४ ॥
Meaning: The tree of the three worlds (Bhur, Bhuvah, Svah), the one who helps cross existence, the Creator, and the Great-Grandfather (of Brahma). The Sacrifice, the Lord of sacrifice, the Performer, the limb of sacrifice, and the vehicle of sacrifice.
Verse 105

यज्ञान्तकृद्यज्ञगुह्यमन्नमन्नाद एव च ॥ १०५ ॥
Meaning: The sustainer of sacrifice, the creator of sacrifice, the lord of sacrifice, the consumer of offerings, and the means of sacrifice. The one who concludes the sacrifice, the secret of sacrifice, the Food, and the Eater of food.
Verse 106
आत्मयोनिः स्वयञ्जातो वैखानः सामगायनः ।
देवकीनन्दनः स्रष्टा क्षितीशः पापनाशनः ॥ १०६ ॥
Meaning: The source of the self, the self-born, the excavator of the universe, and the singer of Samas. The joy of Devaki (Krishna), the Creator, the Lord of the Earth, and the Destroyer of sins.
Verse 107
शङ्खभृन्नन्दकी चक्री शार्ङ्गधन्वा गदाधरः ।
रथाङ्गपाणिरक्षोभ्यः सर्वप्रहरणायुधः ॥ १०७ ॥
Meaning: The wielder of the Conch, the Sword (Nandaka), the Discus, the Bow (Sharnga), and the Mace. The one whose hand is the chariot-wheel, the unshakeable, and the one who uses every element as a weapon for protection.
Verse 108
वनमाली गदी शार्ङ्गी शङ्खी चक्री च नन्दकी ।
श्रीमान्नारायणो विष्णुर्वासुदेवोऽभिरक्षतु ॥ १०८ ॥
Meaning: Adorned with forest garlands, holding the mace, the bow, the conch, the discus, and the sword; may the illustrious Narayana, Vishnu, Vasudeva protect us all.

नमः सर्वहितार्थाय जगदाधारहेतवे ।
साष्टाङ्गोऽयं प्रणामस्ते प्रयत्नेन मया कृतः ॥

Salutations to the Lord, the benefactor of all, the cause and support of the world. I offer this prostration with all my heart.

॥ इति श्रीमहाभारते अनुशासनपर्वणि श्रीविष्णुसहस्रनामस्तोत्रं सम्पूर्णम् ॥

Thus ends the Shri Vishnu Sahasranamam Stotram found in the Mahabharata.

फलश्रुतिः (The Benefits of Recitation)

The Phalashruti (the "listening to the fruits") describes the spiritual, mental, and physical benefits of reciting or hearing the thousand names of Vishnu. It serves as the conclusion to the stotram in the Mahabharata.

इतीदं कीर्तनीयस्य केशवस्य महात्मनः ।
नाम्नां सहस्रं दिव्यानामशेषेण प्रकीर्तितम् ॥
Meaning: Thus, the thousand divine names of the great soul Kesava, who is worthy of all praise, have been sung in their entirety.
य इदं शृणुयान्नित्यं यश्चापि परिकीर्तयेत् ।
नाशुभं प्राप्नुयात् किञ्चित्सोऽमुत्रेह च मानवः ॥
Meaning: That man who hears this daily or recites it shall never meet with anything inauspicious, either in this world or the next.
वेदान्तगो ब्राह्मणः स्यात् क्षत्रियो विजयी भवेत् ।
वैश्यो धनसमृद्धः स्यात् शूद्रः सुखमवाप्नुयात् ॥
Meaning: The seeker of knowledge will gain wisdom; the warrior will gain victory; the businessman will gain wealth; and the worker will gain happiness. (Representing that all paths of life are blessed).
धर्मार्थी प्राप्नुयाद्धर्मं अर्थार्थी चार्थमाप्नुयात् ।
कामानवाप्नुयात्कामी प्रजार्थी प्राप्नुयात्प्रजाम् ॥
Meaning: He who seeks righteousness (Dharma) will find it; he who seeks wealth will gain it; he who seeks pleasures will enjoy them; and he who desires progeny will be blessed with children.
न वासुदेवभक्तानामशुभं विद्यते क्वचित् ।
जन्ममृत्युजराव्याधिभयं नैवोपजायते ॥
Meaning: No evil ever befalls the devotees of Vasudeva. They are freed from the fear of birth, death, old age, and disease.
कायेन वाचा मनसेन्द्रियैर्वा बुद्ध्यात्मना वा प्रकृतेः स्वभावात् ।
करोमि यद्यत्सकलं परस्मै नारायणायेति समर्पयामि ॥
Universal Dedication:
Whatever I perform with my body, speech, mind, senses, intellect, or soul, or by my innate nature—I dedicate all of it to the Supreme Lord Narayana.

श्री ललिता सहस्रनाम

Dhyanam
सिन्दूरारुणविग्रहां त्रिनयनां माणिक्यमौलिस्फुरत्
तारानायकशेखरां स्मितमुखीमापीनवक्षोरुहाम् ।
पाणिभ्यामलिपूर्णरत्नचषकं रक्तोत्पलं बिभ्रतीं
सौम्यां रत्नघटस्थरक्तचरणां ध्यायेत्परामम्बिकाम् ॥
Meaning: I meditate on the Supreme Mother: crimson like vermilion, three-eyed, with a crown of rubies and the moon as her crest. She is smiling, holds a gem-studded cup and a red lotus, and rests her red feet on a vessel of jewels.
Verse 1
श्रीमाता श्रीमहाराज्ञी श्रीमत्सिंहासनेश्वरी ।
चिदग्निकुण्डसम्भूता देवकार्यसमुद्यता ॥ १ ॥
Meaning: Salutations to the Divine Mother (Sri Mata), the Great Empress (Sri Maharajni), and the Sovereign of the Lion-Throne. She who arose from the fire-altar of Pure Consciousness to fulfill the mission of the Gods.
Verse 2
उद्यद्भानुसहस्राभा चतुर्बाहुसमन्विता ।
रागस्वरूपपाशाढ्या क्रोधाकाराङ्कुशोज्ज्वला ॥ २ ॥
Meaning: Radiant as a thousand rising suns, endowed with four arms; She holds the noose (Pasha) representing Desire and the goad (Ankusha) representing Wrath/Restraint.
Verse 3
मनोरूपेक्षुकोदण्डा पञ्चतन्मात्रसायका ।
निजारुणप्रभापूरमज्जद्ब्रह्माण्डमण्डला ॥ ३ ॥
Meaning: She holds the sugarcane bow (the Mind) and the five flower arrows (the Senses). The entire universe is bathed in her glowing red radiance.
Verse 4
चम्पकाशोकपुन्नागसौगन्धिकलसत्कचा ।
कुरुविन्दमणिश्रेणीकनत्कोटीरमण्डिता ॥ ४ ॥
Meaning: Her hair is adorned with Champaka, Ashoka, Punnaga, and Saugandhika flowers. Her crown is brilliant with rows of Kuruvinda rubies.
Verse 5
अष्टमीचन्द्रविभ्राजदलिकस्थलशोभिता ।
मुखचन्द्रकलङ्काभमृगनाभिविशेषका ॥ ५ ॥
Meaning: Her forehead is as beautiful as the moon on the eighth day. The musk tilaka on her face resembles the spot on the moon.
Verse 6
वदनस्मरमाङ्गल्यगृहतोरणचिल्लिका ।
वक्त्रलक्ष्मीपरीवाहचलन्मीनाभलोचना ॥ ६ ॥
Meaning: Her eyebrows are the auspicious archways to the house of the God of Love. Her eyes are like moving fish in the ocean of her radiant face.
Verse 7
नवचम्पकपुष्पाभनासादण्डविराजिता ।
ताराकान्तिकारिण्यनासिकाभरणभासुरा ॥ ७ ॥
Meaning: Her nose is as beautiful as a freshly blown Champaka flower, adorned with a jewel that outshines the stars.
Verse 8
कदम्बमञ्जरीक्लृप्तकर्णपूरमनोहरा ।
ताटङ्कयुगलीभूततपनोडुपमण्डला ॥ ८ ॥
Meaning: Her ears are adorned with Kadamba flowers. Her earrings are the Sun and the Moon themselves.
Verse 9
पद्मरागशिलादर्शपरिभावि कपोलभूः ।
नवविद्रुमबिम्बश्री न्यक्कारि रदनच्छदा ॥ ९ ॥
Meaning: Her cheeks are more polished than rubies. Her lips put the beauty of fresh coral and the Bimba fruit to shame.
Verse 10
शुद्धविद्याङ्कुराकारद्विजपङ्क्तिद्वयोज्ज्वला ।
कर्पूरवीटिकामोदसमाकर्षद्दिगन्तरा ॥ १० ॥
Meaning: Her teeth are like the sprouts of pure knowledge. The fragrance of the betel leaves she chews attracts the world from all directions.
Verse 11
निजसंलापमाधुर्यविनिर्भर्त्सितकच्छपी ।
मन्दस्मितप्रभापूरमज्जत्कामेशमानसा ॥ ११ ॥
Meaning: The sweetness of her voice shames the Veena of Saraswati. Her gentle smile drowns the mind of Lord Kameswara in bliss.
Verse 12
अनाकलितसादृश्यचुबुकश्रीविराजिता ।
कामेशबद्धमाङ्गल्यसूत्रशोभितकन्धरा ॥ १२ ॥
Meaning: Her chin has a beauty beyond comparison. Her neck is adorned with the Mangala-sutra tied by Lord Kameswara.
Verse 13
कनकाङ्गदकेयूरकमनीयभुजान्विता ।
रत्नग्रैवेयचिन्ताकलोलमुक्ताफलान्विता ॥ १३ ॥
Meaning: Her arms are adorned with golden bangles and armlets. Her neck is decorated with ornaments of gems and pearls.
Verse 14
कामेश्वरप्रेमरत्नमणिप्रतिपणस्तनी ।
नाभ्यालवालरोमालिलताफलकुचद्वयी ॥ १४ ॥
Meaning: Her breasts are like gems offered in exchange for the love of Kameswara. They appear as fruit on the vine of the line of hair rising from her navel.
Verse 15
लक्ष्यरौमलताधारसमुन्नेयमध्यमा ।
स्तनभारदलन्मध्यपट्टबन्धवलित्रया ॥ १५ ॥
Meaning: Her waist is so slender it is only inferred by the line of hair. It has three folds like a golden band.
Verse 16
अरुणारुणकौसुम्भ-वस्त्रभास्वत्कटीतटी ।
रत्नकिङ्किणिकारम्य-रशनादामभूषिता ॥ १६ ॥
Meaning: Her waist is draped in a garment as red as the dawn. It is adorned with a belt of tinkling golden bells.
Verse 17
कामेशज्ञातसौभाग्यमार्दवोरुद्वयान्विता ।
माणिक्यमुकुटाकारजानुद्वयविराजिता ॥ १७ ॥
Meaning: The beauty and softness of her thighs are known only to Kameswara. Her knees are like crowns of rubies.
Verse 18
इन्द्रगोपपरिक्षिप्तस्मरतूणाभजङ्घिका ।
गूढगुल्फा कूर्मपृष्ठजयिष्णुप्रपदान्विता ॥ १८ ॥
Meaning: Her calves are like the quiver of the God of Love. Her ankles are hidden, and her feet have the graceful curve of a tortoise shell.
Verse 19
नखदीधितिसञ्छन्ननमज्जनतमोगुणा ।
पदद्वयप्रभाजालपराकृतसरोरुहा ॥ १९ ॥
Meaning: The brilliance of her toe-nails dispels the darkness of ignorance in those who bow. Her feet excel the beauty of the lotus.
Verse 20
शिञ्जानमणिमञ्जीरमण्डितश्रीपदाम्बुजा ।
मरालमान्तगमना महालावण्यशेवधिः ॥ २० ॥
Meaning: Her lotus feet are adorned with tinkling gem-anklets. Her gait is like that of a swan, and she is a treasure-house of beauty.
Verse 21

Meaning: She is entirely rosy-hued, perfect in every limb, and wears all ornaments. She sits on the lap of Shiva-Kameswara and is the auspicious one who holds her husband's heart.
Verse 22
सुमेरुमध्यशृङ्गस्था श्रीमन्नगरनायिका ।

Meaning: She resides on the middle peak of Mount Meru. She is the Queen of the auspicious city (Srinagara) and lives in a house of wish-fulfilling gems.
Verse 23
महापद्माटवीसंस्था कदम्बवनवासिनी ।
सुधासागरमध्यस्था कामाक्षी कामदायिनी ॥ २३ ॥
Meaning: She stays in the great forest of lotuses and resides in the Kadamba grove. She sits in the middle of the ocean of nectar and grants all desires with her loving eyes.
Verse 24
देवर्षिगणसङ्घातस्तूयमानात्मवैभवा ।
भण्डासुरवधोद्युक्तशक्तिसेनासमन्विता ॥ २४ ॥
Meaning: Her glory is praised by the assembly of Devas and Rishis. She is accompanied by an army of Shaktis ready to slay the demon Bhandasura.
Verse 25
सम्पत्करीसमारूढसिन्धुरव्रजसेविता ।
अश्वारूढाधिष्ठिताश्वकोटिकोटिभिरावृता ॥ २५ ॥
Meaning: She is served by Sampatkari atop a fleet of elephants. She is surrounded by crores of horses led by Ashwarudha.
Verse 26
चक्रराजरथारूढसर्वायुधपरिष्कृता ।
गेयचक्ररथारूढमन्त्रिणीपरिसैविता ॥ २६ ॥
Meaning: She is seated in the Chakra-Raja chariot, equipped with all weapons, and is served by Mantrini (Shyamala) who is seated in the Geya-Chakra chariot.
Verse 27
किरिचक्ररथारूढदण्डनाथापुरस्कृता ।
ज्वालामालिनिकाक्षिप्तवह्निप्राकारमध्यगा ॥ २७ ॥
Meaning: She is preceded by Dandanatha (Varahi) on the Kiri-Chakra chariot. She stays in the center of the rampart of fire created by Jvalamalinika.
Verse 28
भण्डसैन्यवधोद्युक्तशक्तिविक्रमहर्षिता ।
नित्यापराक्रमाटोपनिरीक्षणसमुत्सुका ॥ २८ ॥
Meaning: She rejoices at the valor of the Shaktis intent on destroying Bhandasura's army. She eagerly watches the pride and prowess of the Nitya deities.
Verse 29
भण्डपुत्रवधोद्युक्तबालाविक्रमनन्दिता ।
मन्त्रिण्यम्बाविरचितविषङ्गवधतोषिता ॥ २९ ॥
Meaning: She is delighted by the bravery of Bala (her daughter) in killing the sons of Bhanda. She is pleased by the slaying of Vishanga by Mantrini.
Verse 30
विशुक्रप्राणहरणवाराहीवीर्यनन्दिता ।
कामेश्वरमुखालोककल्पितश्रीगणेश्वरा ॥ ३० ॥
Meaning: She rejoices in the valor of Varahi, who took the life of Vishukra. By a mere glance at the face of Lord Kameswara, she brought forth Lord Ganesha.
Verse 31
महागणेशनिर्भिन्नविघ्नयन्त्रप्रहर्षिता ।
भण्डासुरेन्द्रनिर्मुक्तशस्त्रप्रत्यस्त्रवर्षिणी ॥ ३१ ॥
Meaning: She is thrilled to see Ganesha shatter the Yantra of obstacles. She rains down counter-missiles against every weapon launched by Bhandasura.
Verse 32
कराङ्गुलिनखोत्पन्ननारायणदशाकृतिः ।
महापाशुपतास्त्राग्निनिर्दग्धासुरसैनिक ॥ ३२ ॥
Meaning: From the nails of her ten fingers, she created the ten incarnations of Narayana. She burned the demon army with the fire of the Mahapashupata weapon.
Verse 33
कामेश्वरास्त्रनिर्दग्धसभण्डासुरशून्यका ।
ब्रह्मोपेन्द्रमहेन्द्रादिदेवसंस्तुतवैभवा ॥ ३३ ॥
Meaning: She destroyed Bhandasura and his city (Shunyaka) with the Kameswara weapon. Her glory is sung by Brahma, Vishnu, Indra, and all the gods.
Verse 34
हरनेत्राग्निसन्दग्धकामसञ्जीवनौषधिः ।
श्रीमद्वाग्भवकूटैकस्वरूपमुखपङ्कजा ॥ ३४ ॥
Meaning: She is the life-giving medicine to Manmatha (Kama), who was burned by the fire of Shiva's eye. Her lotus face represents the Vagbhava-kuta (the first part of the Panchadasi mantra).
Verse 35
कण्ठाधःकटिपर्यन्त मध्यकूटस्वरूपिणी ।
शक्तिकूटैकतापन्नकट्यधोभागधारिणी ॥ ३५ ॥
Meaning: From her neck to her waist, she is the Madhyama-kuta. From her waist downwards, she embodies the Shakti-kuta of the sacred mantra.
Verse 36
मूलमन्त्रात्मिका मूलकूटत्रयकलेबरा ।
कुलामृतैकरसिका कुलसङ्केतपालिनी ॥ ३६ ॥
Meaning: She is the soul of the root mantra (Mula-mantra), and her body is the three parts of that mantra. She enjoys the nectar of the Kula (Sahasrara) and protects the secret traditions.
Verse 37
कुलाङ्गना कुलान्तस्था कौलिनी कुलयोगिनी ।
अकुला समयान्तस्था समयाचारतत्परा ॥ ३७ ॥
Meaning: She is the Lady of the Kula, residing within it. She is Kaulini, the yogini of Kula. She is also 'Akula' (beyond) and resides in the Samaya path of worship.
Verse 38
मूलाधारैकनिलया ब्रह्मग्रन्थिविभेदिनी ।
मणिपूरान्तरुदिता विष्णुग्रन्थिविभेदिनी ॥ ३८ ॥
Meaning: She resides in the Muladhara chakra. She pierces the Brahma-granthi (knot of creation). She appears in the Manipura and pierces the Vishnu-granthi (knot of preservation).
Verse 39
आज्ञाचक्रान्तरालस्था रुद्रग्रन्थिविभेदिनी ।
सहस्राराम्बुजारूढा सुधासाराभिवर्षिणी ॥ ३९ ॥
Meaning: She resides in the Ajna chakra and pierces the Rudra-granthi (knot of dissolution). She then ascends to the Sahasrara (thousand-petaled lotus) and showers the nectar of bliss.
Verse 40
तटिल्लेखातनुचि रुचिः षट्चक्रोपरि संस्थिता ।
महासक्तिः कुण्डलिनी बिसतन्तुतनीयसी ॥ ४० ॥
Meaning: Radiant like a flash of lightning, she stands above the six chakras. She is the Great Power, Kundalini, as slender as the fiber of a lotus stalk.
Verse 41
भवानी भावनागम्या भवारण्यकुठारिका ।
भद्रप्रिया भद्रमूर्तिर्भवक्तप्रियङ्करी ॥ ४१ ॥
Meaning: She is Bhavani, reachable through meditation. She is the axe that cuts down the forest of rebirth. She loves and embodies auspiciousness, bringing joy to her devotees.
Verse 42
भक्तिप्रिया भक्तिगम्या भक्तिवश्या भयापहा ।
शाम्भवी शारदाराध्या शर्वाणी शर्मदायिनी ॥ ४२ ॥
Meaning: She loves devotion and is reached by it; she is won by devotion and removes all fear. She is Shambhavi, worshipped by Sharada (Saraswati), and the giver of happiness.
Verse 43
शाङ्करी श्रीकरी साध्वी शरच्चन्द्रनिभानना ।
शातोदरी शान्तिमती निराधारा निरञ्जना ॥ ४३ ॥
Meaning: She is the consort of Shankara, the giver of prosperity, and the virtuous one. Her face is like the autumn moon. She is slender-waisted, peaceful, without support, and stainless.
Verse 44

निर्गुणा निष्कला शान्ता निष्कामा निरुपप्लवा ॥ ४४ ॥
Meaning: She is untouched by actions, pure, eternal, formless, and untroubled. She is beyond attributes, indivisible, calm, desireless, and indestructible.
Verse 45
निष्कलंका निरुपाधिर्निरीश्वरा नीरागिणी ।
रागमथनी निर्मदा मदनाशिनी ॥ ४५ ॥
Meaning: She is faultless, without limitation, and has no master. She is without passion, the destroyer of attachments, free from pride, and the slayer of ego.
Verse 46
निश्चिन्ता निरहङ्कारा निर्मोहा मोहनाशिनी ।
निर्ममा ममहन्त्री च निष्पापा पापनाशिनी ॥ ४६ ॥
Meaning: She is free from all worries and ego. She is without delusion and is the destroyer of delusion. She has no sense of "mine" (attachment) and is the destroyer of selfishness and sin.
Verse 47
निष्क्रोधा क्रोधशमनी निर्लोभा लोभनाशिनी ।
निःसंशया संशयघ्नी निर्भवा भवनाशिनी ॥ ४७ ॥
Meaning: She is without anger and quells anger in others. She is without greed and destroys greed. She is without doubt, the dispeller of doubts, and the one who ends the cycle of rebirth.
Verse 48
निर्विकल्पा निराबाधा निर्भेदा भेदनाशिनी ।
निर्नाशा मृत्युमथनी निष्क्रिया निष्परिग्रहा ॥ ४८ ॥
Meaning: She is beyond change and remains undisturbed. She sees no duality and destroys the sense of separation. She is immortal, the conqueror of death, actionless, and possesses nothing.
Verse 49
निस्तुला नीलचिकुरा निरपाया निरत्यया ।
दुर्लभा दुर्गमा दुर्गा दुःखापहा सुखप्रदा ॥ ४९ ॥
Meaning: She is incomparable, with dark blue hair. She is beyond danger and indestructible. She is hard to attain, difficult to reach, and is Durga herself—the remover of sorrow and giver of happiness.
Verse 50
दुष्टदूरा दुराचारशमनी दोषवर्जिता ।
सर्वज्ञा सान्द्रकरुणा समानाधिकवर्जिता ॥ ५० ॥
Meaning: She is far from the wicked and puts an end to evil conduct. She is faultless, all-knowing, filled with intense compassion, and has no equal or superior.
Verse 51
सर्वशक्तिमयी सर्वमङ्गला सद्गतिप्रदा ।
सर्वेश्वरी सर्वमयी सर्वमन्त्रस्वरूपिणी ॥ ५१ ॥
Meaning: She possesses all powers and is all-auspicious. She guides us to the right path. She is the Empress of all, the essence of everything, and the form of all mantras.
Verse 52

माहेश्वरी महादेवी महालक्ष्मीर्मृडप्रिया ॥ ५२ ॥
Meaning: She is the soul of all Yantras and the form of all Tantras. She is the state of Mind-transcendence. She is the Great Empress, the Great Goddess, Lakshmi, and the beloved of Shiva.
Verse 53
महारूपा महापूज्या महापतकनाशिनी ।
महामाया महासत्त्वा महाशक्तिर्महारतिः ॥ ५३ ॥
Meaning: She has a magnificent form and is most worthy of worship. She destroys great sins. She is the Great Illusion, the Great Reality, the Great Power, and the Great Bliss.
Verse 54
महाभोगा महैश्वर्या महावीर्या महाबला ।
महाबुद्धिर्महासिक्धिर्महायोगेश्वरेश्वरी ॥ ५४ ॥
Meaning: She is the source of great enjoyment and great wealth. She is of great valor, great strength, great intelligence, and great success. She is the Queen of the Lords of Yoga.
Verse 55
महातन्त्रा महामन्त्रा महायन्त्रा महासना ।
महायागक्रमाराध्या महाभैरवपूजिता ॥ ५५ ॥
Meaning: She is the Great Tantra, Mantra, and Yantra. She is seated on a great throne and is worshipped through great rituals. She is worshipped by Mahabhairava (Shiva).
Verse 56
महेश्वरमहाकल्पमहाताण्डवसाक्षिणी ।
महाकामेशमहिषी महात्रिपुरसुन्दरी ॥ ५६ ॥
Meaning: She is the witness to the great dance of Shiva at the end of the cosmic cycle. She is the Queen of the great Kameswara and the Great Tripura Sundari.
Verse 57
चतुष्षष्ट्युपचाराढ्या चतुष्षष्टिकलामयी ।
महाचतुष्षष्टिकोटियोगिनीगणसेविता ॥ ५७ ॥
Meaning: She is worshipped with 64 types of offerings and embodies the 64 arts. She is served by 64 crores of Yoginis.
Verse 58
मनुविद्या चन्द्रविद्या चन्द्रमण्डलमध्यगा ।
चारुरूपा चारुहासा चारुचन्द्रकलाधरा ॥ ५८ ॥
Meaning: She is the knowledge of Manu and Chandra. She resides in the orb of the moon. She has a beautiful form, a beautiful smile, and wears the beautiful crescent moon.
Verse 59
चराचरजगन्नाथ चक्रराजनिकेतना ।
पार्वती पद्मनयना पद्मरागसमप्रभा ॥ ५९ ॥
Meaning: She is the Mistress of all moving and unmoving things. Her home is the Sri Chakra. She is Parvati, the lotus-eyed one, with the brilliance of a ruby.
Verse 60
पञ्चप्रेतासनासीना पञ्चब्रह्मस्वरूपिणी ।
चिन्मयी परमानन्दा विज्ञानघनरूपिणी ॥ ६० ॥
Meaning: She is seated on a throne formed by the five deities (Brahma, Vishnu, Rudra, Ishwara, and Sadashiva). She is pure consciousness, supreme bliss, and the embodiment of wisdom.
Verse 61
ध्यानध्यातृध्येयरूपा धर्माधर्मविवर्जिता ।
विश्वरूपा जागरणवती स्वपन्ती तैजसात्मिका ॥ ६१ ॥
Meaning: She is the meditation, the meditator, and the object of meditation. She is beyond virtue and vice. She is the Universal Form, the waking state, and the dreaming state (Taijasa).
Verse 62
सुप्ता प्राज्ञात्मिका तुर्या सर्वावस्थाविवर्जिता ।
सृष्टिकर्त्री ब्रह्मरूपा गोप्त्री गोविन्दरूपिणी ॥ ६२ ॥
Meaning: She is the deep sleep state (Prajna) and the fourth state of pure consciousness (Turiya). She creates as Brahma and protects as Govinda (Vishnu).
Verse 63
संहारिणी रुद्ररूपा तिरोधानकरीश्वरी ।
सदाशिवाऽनुग्रहदा पञ्चकृत्यपरायणा ॥ ६३ ॥
Meaning: She destroys as Rudra and conceals as Ishwara. She grants grace as Sadashiva, performing the five cosmic acts.
Verse 64
भानुमण्डलमध्यस्था भैरवी भगमालिनी ।
पद्मासना भगवती पद्मनाभसहोदरी ॥ ६४ ॥
Meaning: She resides in the center of the solar orb. She is Bhairavi, Bhagamalini, seated on a lotus. She is the Goddess who is the sister of Vishnu (Padmanabha).
Verse 65
उन्मेषनिमिषोत्पन्नविपन्नभुवनावली ।
सहस्रशीर्षवदना सहस्राक्षी सहस्रपात् ॥ ६५ ॥
Meaning: Universes are created when she opens her eyes and destroyed when she closes them. She has a thousand heads, a thousand eyes, and a thousand feet (omnipresence).
Verse 66
आब्रह्मकीटजननी वर्णाश्रमविधायिनी ।
निजाज्ञाप्यरूपनिगमा पुण्यापुण्यफलप्रदा ॥ ६६ ॥
Meaning: She is the mother of all, from Brahma to the smallest insect. She established the social and spiritual orders. The Vedas are Her commands, and She dispenses the fruits of both merit and sin.
Verse 67
श्रुतिसीमन्तसिन्दूरीकृतपादाब्जधूलिका ।
सकलागमसन्दोहशुक्तिसम्पुटमौक्तिका ॥ ६७ ॥
Meaning: The dust of Her lotus feet is the vermilion in the parting hair of the Vedas (personified). She is the pearl within the shell of all the Agamas (sacred texts).
Verse 68
पुरुषार्थप्रदा पूर्णा भोगिनी भुवनेश्वरी ।
अम्बिकाऽनादिनिधना हरब्रह्मेन्द्रसेविता ॥ ६८ ॥
Meaning: She grants the four goals of life (Dharma, Artha, Kama, Moksha). She is complete, the enjoyer, and the Ruler of the Universe. She is the Mother without beginning or end, served by Shiva, Brahma, and Indra.
Verse 69
नारायणी नादरूपा नामरूपविवर्जिता ।
ह्रीङ्कारी ह्रीमती हृद्या हेयोपादेयवर्जिता ॥ ६९ ॥
Meaning: She is Narayani, the essence of sound (Nada). She is beyond name and form. She is the syllable 'Hrim', possessed of modesty, residing in the heart, and beyond what is rejected or accepted.
Verse 70
राजराजार्चिता राज्ञी रम्या राजीवलोचना ।
रञ्जनी रमणी रस्या रणत्किङ्किणिमेखला ॥ ७० ॥
Meaning: She is worshipped by Kubera (King of Kings). She is the Queen, the beautiful one with lotus eyes. She gives delight, is charming, is the essence of bliss, and wears a girdle of tinkling bells.
Verse 71
रमा राकेन्दुवदना रतिरूपा रतिप्रिया ।
रक्षाकरी राक्षसघ्नी रामा रमणलम्पटा ॥ ७१ ॥
Meaning: She is Lakshmi (Rama), with a face like the full moon. She is the form of Rati (beauty/desire) and loves Rati. She is the Protectress, the slayer of demons, and the enchanting woman who delights in Her Lord.
Verse 72
काम्या कामकलारूपा कदम्बकुसुमप्रिया ।
कल्याणी जगतीकन्दा करुणारससागरा ॥ ७२ ॥
Meaning: She is the object of desire and the form of Kama-kala. She loves Kadamba flowers. She is auspiciousness, the root of the universe, and an ocean of compassion.
Verse 73
कलावती कलालापा कान्ता कादम्बरीप्रिया ।
वरदा वामनयना वारुणीमदविह्वला ॥ ७३ ॥
Meaning: She possesses all arts and Her speech is artistic. She is the beloved, fond of mead (spiritual intoxication). She is the giver of boons, the beautiful-eyed one, and intoxicated by Varuni.
Verse 74
विश्वाधिका वेदवेद्या विन्ध्याचलनिवासिनी ।
विधात्री वेदजननी विष्णुमाया विलासिनी ॥ ७४ ॥
Meaning: She transcends the universe and is known through the Vedas. She dwells in the Vindhya mountains. She is the creator, the mother of the Vedas, the illusory power of Vishnu, and the playful one.

Verse 183
नारायणी नादरूपा नामरूपविवर्जिता ।
ह्रीङ्कारी ह्रीमती हृद्या हेयोपादेयवर्जिता ॥ १८३ ॥
Meaning: She is Narayani, the essence of sound. She is beyond name and form. She is the sacred syllable Hrim, residing in the heart, beyond all dualities of acceptance or rejection.
॥ इति श्रीब्रह्माण्डपुराणे उत्तरखण्डे श्रीललितासहस्रनामस्तोत्रं सम्पूर्णम् ॥
(Thus concludes the Shri Lalita Sahasranama Stotram from the Brahmanda Purana.)
Phalashruti 1
इत्येतन्नामसाहस्रं कथितं ते घटोद्भव ।
रहस्यानां रहस्यं च ललिताप्रीतिदायकम् ॥ १ ॥
Meaning: Thus, O Agastya (born from a pot), I have narrated to you these thousand names. This is the secret of all secrets and is most pleasing to Goddess Lalita.
Phalashruti 2
अनेन सदृशं स्तोत्रं न भूतं न भविष्यति ।
सर्वरोगप्रशमनं सर्वसम्पत्प्रवर्धनम् ॥ २ ॥
Meaning: A hymn equal to this never existed in the past, nor will there be one in the future. It cures all diseases and increases all forms of wealth and prosperity.
Phalashruti 3
सर्वापन्मृत्युशमनं कालमृत्युनिवारणम् ।
सर्वज्वरार्तिशमनं दीर्घायुष्यप्रदायकम् ॥ ३ ॥
Meaning: It averts accidental deaths and guards against untimely death. It relieves the pain of all fevers and bestows a long, healthy life.
Phalashruti 4
पुत्रपौत्रप्रदं चैव चतुर्वर्गफलप्रदम् ।
इदं विशेषाल्ललिता-नामसाहस्रमुत्तमम् ॥ ४ ॥
Meaning: It grants children and grandchildren, and bestows the four-fold fruits of life (Dharma, Artha, Kama, and Moksha). This Sahasranamam of Lalita is indeed supreme.
Phalashruti 5
यः पठेन्नामसाहस्रं सकृदप्यतन्द्रितः ।
तस्य पुण्यफलं वक्तुं न शक्नोति विनायकः ॥ ५ ॥
Meaning: Even Lord Ganesha (Vinayaka) cannot fully describe the merit gained by one who chants these thousand names even once with devotion.
॥ श्री ललिताम्बिकार्पणमस्तु ॥
(May all this be offered to the Divine Mother Sri Lalitambika.)
